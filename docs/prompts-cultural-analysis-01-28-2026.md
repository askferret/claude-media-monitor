# Test Prompts for Business Anthropology & Cultural Analysis

**Date:** 01/28/2026

Curated prompts and queries for testing the market intelligence system from a **business anthropology and cultural analyst** lens: consumer and market intelligence, cultural analysis, trends, signals, and cultural forecasting.

## Quick Test (One Command)

Try these in Cursor chat with **`/query [topic]`**:

| Prompt | What it surfaces |
|--------|------------------|
| **consumer culture and identity** | Identity-driven consumption, belonging, self-expression through brands |
| **cultural trends forecasting** | Trend reports, foresight, cultural forecasting methods and outputs |
| **ritual and consumption** | Rituals, habits, occasions—anthropology of consumption |
| **generational consumer behavior** | Gen Z, millennials, Gen Alpha—values, media, spending |
| **subculture and mainstream** | Subcultural adoption, mainstreaming, cultural diffusion |
| **values shift consumer** | Changing values (sustainability, authenticity, wellness) and market impact |
| **experiential economy** | Experiences over things, events, immersive consumption |
| **nostalgia economy** | Retro, revival, heritage—nostalgia as driver of demand |
| **wellness culture market** | Wellness as culture and category—mind, body, community |
| **semiotics branding** | Meaning, symbolism, signs in branding and consumer interpretation |
| **cultural signals emerging** | Early signals, weak signals, cultural emergence |
| **anthropology of markets** | Markets as cultural systems, exchange, meaning-making |

## Suggested First Three Tests

1. **Lightweight**  
   `/query consumer culture and identity`  
   Fast run; good for checking articles → signals → citations.

2. **Cultural + commercial**  
   `/query --config queries/example_cultural_trends_consumer.json`  
   Broader sweep with your focus areas and signal types.

3. **Forward-looking**  
   `/query --config queries/example_cultural_forecasting_signals.json`  
   Emphasis on forecasting and emerging signals.

After each run, use **`/signals --topic "…"`** or **`/signals --recent 7`** to review outputs. Adjust search terms and focus areas in the JSON configs to match your projects.
